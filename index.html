Why Red Sandalwood?

Nature's Most
Precious Timber

Red Sandalwood (Pterocarpus santalinus) is endemic to the Eastern Ghats of Andhra Pradesh and among the world's most sought-after luxury woods — used in high-end furniture, Ayurvedic medicine, cosmetics, and spiritual artefacts. Its export value makes it a multi-crore asset per acre at maturity.

  • ✦ Protected species with government-regulated cultivation
  • ✦ International demand from Japan, China, and the Middle East
  • ✦ Appreciates 10–15× in value over the growth cycle
  • ✦ Drought-resistant, low-maintenance crop
